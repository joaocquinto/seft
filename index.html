Welcome

This page presents the Symbolic Emotional Fusion Theory (SEFT), a formally computable model rooted in symbolic affective logic for identifying authentic affective bonds. Explore the research, download the full article, and get in touch if you'd like to collaborate on emotional AI, symbolic cognition, or affective simulations.
